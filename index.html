Bio

I completed my Master's and Ph.D. at Computer Game and Intelligence (CGI) Lab under the guidance of Professor I-Chen Wu at National YangMing ChiaoTung University, Taiwan.

I'm interested in applying Artificial Intelligence and Deep Reinforcement Learning to Computer Games, Robotics, and Autonomous Driving

My research focuses on object representation for robotic manipulation, developing AI/Deep RL to achieve human-level performance in video games, and optimizing smooth control policies for autonomous vehicles.
