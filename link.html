Hoang-Giang Cao (高黃江)

I work on AI/Deep Reinforcement Learning for Computer Game, Robotics, and Autonomous Driving.

My research focuses on object representation for robotic manipulation, developing AI/Deep RL to achieve human-level performance in video games, and optimizing smooth control policies for autonomous vehicles.

I did my PhD at Computer Game and Intelligence (CGI) Lab with Professor I-Chen Wu. National YangMing ChiaoTung University, Taiwan.
